About me

Hi there, Fatah here (发达奇拉). @x86fatah is my alias. I'm based in Malaysia, working as a computer security specialist at a local company. I'm not the most talkative person if it is not important, I value objectivity.
Specializing in Software Research, Analysis, and Development (Offensive and Defensive) on the Daily Job and Personal Projects. Focusing on Cross-Platform and Windows Analysis, with an Interest in Reverse Engineering. My previous research involved exploring Windows MalDev antivirus evasion for research purposes, but now I'm more into low-level programming and systems.
Driven by a deep understanding of how computers function at their core. This passion translates into my proficiency in languages like Assembly, C, C++, PowerShell, Python, and Bash.
